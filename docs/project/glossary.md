# Glossary

- Clip: A looping sequence of steps.
- Step: A timed slot in a clip with base note, velocity, gate, probability, octave, and CC data.
- Step lock: A sparse override applied to one step.
- Pitch behavior: A MIDI rule triggered by a specific pitch or pitch class.
- Gate length: Duration between note-on and note-off in beats.
- AUv3: Audio Unit v3 app extension used by hosts on iPadOS and macOS.
- MIDI processor: A plugin that transforms or generates MIDI rather than audio.
